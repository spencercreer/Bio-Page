Student Bio

Spencer William Creer

I was born in Castro Valley, California. I moved to Utah at age seven and have lived in Spanish Fork and Cedar Hills. I graduated with honors and a 4.0 GPA from Lone Peak high school. At age nineteen, I served a two year mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints in southern Germany. I graduated with a bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from Brigham Young University.

I have spent the majority of my career in business and sales roles. From 2014 to 2018, I worked as a sales manager for New Star Communications, an At&t and DirecTv dealer. I led sales teams in Texas, Missouri, and Ohio. After graduating from BYU, I have combined my sales and engineering background in multiple technical sales roles. I currently work as a Sales Engineer for Moxtek, Inc.
